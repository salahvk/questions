CURRENT AFFAIRS — MONTHLY EDITIONS (category 36)
================================================
36. ആനുകാലികം / Current Affairs

Required manifest (lists all uploaded months):
  current_affairs_manifest.json

One file per month (add a new entry to the manifest for each):
  current_affairs_2026_04.json   → April 2026
  current_affairs_2026_06.json   → June 2026
  current_affairs_2026_07.json   → July 2026
  ...pattern: current_affairs_YYYY_MM.json

When you upload a new month:
1. Create current_affairs_YYYY_MM.json with questions
2. Add an edition entry to current_affairs_manifest.json
3. The app will show that month in the picker automatically
